Rules
  1. The RUN21 points system is based on 2 facets: per km made (accumulated) and per month
  2. The runner who reaches 2021kms first receives the grand prize of 50 points, the second 25 points and the third 12 points
  3. The runner who reaches a 200kms multiple number first receives 3 points, the second 2 points and the third 1 point
  4. The runner who does the most kms in a month receives 5 points, the second receives 3 points and the third receives 1. Points are awarded at midnight on the last day of the month.
